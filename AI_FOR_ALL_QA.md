## 2Ô∏è‚É£ What is Your Solution?

**BizNova** is a FREE, AI-powered business management platform designed specifically for small Indian retailers.

### Core Solution Features:

**1. AI Bill Scanner (Saves 15 min/bill)**
- Scan wholesale bills with phone camera
- AI extracts all items, quantities, prices automatically
- One-click to add entire bill to inventory
- Supports Hindi, English, and mixed language bills

**2. Voice-Controlled AI Assistant (Multilingual)**
- Speak naturally: "Show today's sales" or "Add 50 rice bags"
- Works in 5 languages: English, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada
- Voice input AND output for illiterate/semi-literate users
- Instant business insights: "Which items are low in stock?"

**3. Smart Inventory Management**
- Real-time stock tracking with low stock alerts
- Automatic profit calculation (selling price - cost price)
- Supports fractional quantities (0.5 kg, 2.5 liters)
- Category-wise organization

**4. Customer Shopping Assistant**
- Customers can order 24/7 even when shop is closed
- AI suggests items: "I want to make chicken curry for 4 people"
- Shows TOP 3 essential ingredients from shop's inventory
- Reduces retailer's time by handling routine orders

**5. Business Analytics**
- Real-time profit tracking
- Best-selling products identification
- Revenue trends and insights
- Expense tracking with profit analysis
